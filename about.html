About Us

What is CodeSloth-Studio?

We are a software development firm that develops custom software solutions from websites, web apps, to mobile apps for businesses and organizations to streamline their process and elevate their digital transformation and branding. We are trusted by businesses and organizations to small and medium-sized enterprises and also delivered projects from small scale websites to large scale complex systems. We provide fast, affordable and impactful solutions that help our clients' businesses grow. Our team of experts and developers are using the latest tools and technologies which guarantee that the solutions delivered exceed expectations.







Our Story

CodeSloth-Studio

CodeSloth-Studio was founded on May 17, 2020 by Charlou Ybarley together with his group members. This is a website project given by our instructor in Human-Computer Interaction, Ms. Renaline Liwaliw. On May 16, 2022, it was created by Group 2 with 29 members for our final presentation on human-computer interaction. This project was created to help us improve our abilities as IT students and apply what we've acquired during the year. It was also a chance for us to put our skills to the test in terms of designing user interfaces that prioritize efficiency and accessibility. As a result, we created a website that enables a user's interaction simple and clear, involving minimal service in exchange for the most intended result.
